Economic Forecasts

Base Scenario

Using an income growth rate of 3.0 percent as forecasted in 2008

Pro-Poor Scenario

In this scenario, the bottom 25 percent receive an amount of 6.909 dollars corresponding to the cost of mean electricity consumption (53.146KW) observed in 2014

Pro-Poor & Middle-Class

This scenario stimulate the economy by providing fund to the bottom 25 percent as in the pro-poor scenario, and in addition, it distributes half of the cost of the mean electricity annually consumed to the households belonging to the income class 25-50 percent
